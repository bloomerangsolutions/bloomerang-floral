Wedding & Event Flowers in Sarasota

Your wedding day deserves flowers that take your breath away. Bloomerang Floral is one of Sarasota's premier wedding florists, creating stunning floral designs for ceremonies and receptions at venues across the Gulf Coast. From your first consultation to the last petal placed, our designers work side-by-side with you to bring your vision to life.

How Our Wedding Process Works

We believe great wedding flowers start with listening. Here's what working with us looks like:

  1. Free Consultation — We meet in person or via video call to discuss your vision, color palette, venue, and budget. Bring your Pinterest boards, fabric swatches, photos — anything that inspires you.
  2. Custom Proposal — Within 48 hours, you'll receive a detailed floral proposal with itemized pricing, mood boards, and flower recommendations tailored to your wedding date and season.
  3. Design Refinement — We refine every detail together. Swap out a flower, adjust a color, add a ceremony piece — until it's perfect.
  4. Mock-Up (Premium Package) — For our full-service clients, we create a physical mock-up of your bridal bouquet and a sample centerpiece so you can see and feel the design before the big day.
  5. Delivery & Setup — Our team handles delivery, setup, and styling at your venue. We arrive early, place every arrangement, and make sure everything is flawless before guests arrive.
  6. Breakdown & Donation — After the reception, we handle breakdown. We can also arrange for flowers to be donated to local Sarasota hospitals and care homes.

Frequently Asked Questions About Wedding Flowers

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 4–8 months before your wedding date, especially for peak season (October–May in Sarasota). For holiday weekends and Valentine's Day weddings, 6–12 months is ideal. That said, we've pulled off beautiful weddings with 4 weeks notice — reach out and we'll do our best.

Can I see my flowers before the wedding day?

Our Premium and Luxe packages include a physical mock-up of your bridal bouquet. For Essential packages, we provide detailed mood boards and photo references so you know exactly what to expect.

What happens if my preferred flower isn't in season?

We'll discuss seasonality during your consultation and recommend gorgeous alternatives that achieve the same look and feel. Sarasota's tropical climate gives us access to a wider variety than most regions, and we work with specialty growers for imported blooms when needed.

Do you set up and break down at the venue?

Premium and Luxe packages include full setup and breakdown. Essential package includes delivery with basic placement — we'll coordinate with your venue's event staff.
