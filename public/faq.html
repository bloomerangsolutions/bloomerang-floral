Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day flower delivery in Sarasota?

Yes! We offer same-day flower delivery throughout Sarasota and surrounding areas including Siesta Key, Longboat Key, Lakewood Ranch, and Bradenton. Place your order by 2:00 PM for guaranteed same-day delivery.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to Sarasota, Siesta Key, Longboat Key, Lido Key, Bird Key, St. Armands, Lakewood Ranch, Bradenton, Palmetto, Venice, Osprey, Nokomis, Englewood, North Port, University Park, and many more communities throughout the Sarasota-Manatee County area. Contact us if you're unsure about your delivery address.

How do I order flowers by phone?

Simply call us at (941) 806-1911. Our AI-powered voice assistant is available 24/7 to help you choose the perfect arrangement, schedule delivery, and process secure payment — all over the phone.

Can I schedule a delivery for a future date?

Absolutely! During checkout, you can select any future delivery date. We recommend ordering at least 2 days in advance for holidays like Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and Christmas.

What is your substitution policy?

Due to seasonal availability, minor substitutions may occasionally be necessary. We always substitute with flowers of equal or greater value and maintain the style, color scheme, and overall feel of your selected arrangement.

Do you deliver to hospitals and nursing homes?

Yes, we deliver to all major hospitals, nursing homes, and assisted living facilities in the Sarasota area, including Sarasota Memorial Hospital, Doctors Hospital, and all Lakewood Ranch Medical facilities.

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, most of our arrangements last 7-10 days. We include flower food and care instructions with every delivery. Check our Flower Care Guide for detailed tips on extending the life of your blooms.

Do you offer wedding and event flowers?

Yes! We're one of Sarasota's premier wedding florists. We offer free consultations and work with venues across Sarasota, Siesta Key, Longboat Key, The Ringling, and more. Visit our Weddings & Events page for details.

Can I add chocolates or gifts to my flower order?

Of course! We offer a curated selection of add-ons including gourmet chocolates, stuffed animals, balloons, candles, and gift baskets. Add them during checkout or let our phone assistant help you.

What is your return and refund policy?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your arrangement, contact us within 24 hours and we'll either replace the arrangement or issue a full refund.

Still Have Questions?

Contact us at (941) 806-1911 or send us a message. Our team and AI assistant are here to help 24/7. You can also browse our flower care guide, check delivery areas, or start shopping.
